## Potential Limitations

### The ladder has a ceiling

Difficulty is graded by the hardest human technique required, but the ladder tops out at simple coloring and W-Wings. Everything beyond — chains, ALS, uniqueness arguments — lands in a single "extreme" bucket, so one extreme puzzle can be noticeably harder than another. Finer grading would need a full chain solver or an SE-style rating.

### Grading models one solver, not every human

The ladder always applies the cheapest available technique, the way most tutorials teach. A human who spots patterns in a different order may experience a puzzle as slightly easier or harder than its grade.
